How we help

Here at Health Check, we like to see our customers happy. We offer a mood tracker that allows you to monitor your mood and pain levels throughout the day and an excersize and food tracker. You can log all instances of your food intake and excersize to keep up a regular pattern. Our Weight and Inches section logs your weight and inches lost/gained and keeps a history so you can see your progress. Have any extra notes you'd like to keep regarding your health? Thanks to our Note Pad you can record any information you might want to remember, for personal use or your next doctors visit. Think you might forget to check in with us at Health Check? Download our app and you can set up an alarm to remind you to take your medications, or keep up with your logs!
